### Ingredients

- Pork Chops
- salt
- pepper
- paprika
- oregano
- red pepper flakes

1. Season Pork Chops
2. Bake at 400 degrees 20 minutes
3. Flip chops bake 20-30 mins
4. Remove and let sit for 10-20 mins
